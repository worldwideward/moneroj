Tips for a better article:


All fields are required. Write on a notepad and save locally your article before submitting, otherwise you might lose all your work. Use simple sentences for the Subtitle, five words at maximum. Select any thumbnail image, this serves only as a decoration for the article, it doesn't have to be related to the article. You can use HTML tags to format the article:

  • For paragraphs <p style='font-size: 20px;'></p>
  • For external images <image src='ADDRESS' width='100%'>
  • To centralize text and images <center></center>
  • For bold text <b></b>
  • Line break <br>
  • External links <a href='ADDRESS' target='_blank'></a>
  • Title and headers <h1></h1> to <h6></h6>
  • Always use single quotes ' instead of double quotes " inside HTML tags.
  • Images must always have width='100%', or the design will break.
  • Try not to use images in the middle of the first paragraph of the article. Use them either at the beginning or after the first paragraph.
