About this website

Revuo Moneroj was created to help people understand Monero, track its adoption, and compare it objectively with other digital currencies — like Bitcoin. This project is non-profit, funded entirely out of pocket, and driven by a passion for financial privacy and sound money.

If you find the content valuable, consider supporting the project with an XMR donation (using the Trocador widget on this page). All suggestions and feedback are welcome!

We believe Monero is unlike any other cryptocurrency because:

- True Privacy by Default – Optional privacy is no privacy at all. Monero ensures all transactions are confidential and untraceable.

- A Reliable Store of Value – With a low, fixed inflation rate, Monero maintains scarcity while remaining usable as money.

- Real Fungibility – Unlike Bitcoin, where coins carry traceable histories, every XMR is identical and interchangeable.

Monero isn’t just another coin—it’s the only digital cash that delivers on the original promise of cryptocurrency.
