Help about the chart
These charts show the total marketcap divided by the total number of users in /Bitcoin and /Monero subreddits. It makes sense to obeserve this metric, since a price too high in dollars/user might indicate a bubble. Similarly, a low marketcap/user might indicate a bottom.
